Our Journey: From Dream to Destination

Discover the rich history, dedication, and impact that define Lanka Pub Zoo today.

The Legacy Begins (1999)

The story of Lanka Pub Zoo began in 1999, not with a government initiative, but with the personal vision and dedication of my father. He saw a vast, untouched patch of Maho forest and envisioned a sanctuary—a place where the rich biodiversity of Sri Lanka could be protected, studied, and appreciated by all.

The initial phase was driven by a pioneering spirit, focusing on rescue and rehabilitation of native animals. My father's commitment and hands-on approach soon earned the trust of local conservationists and authorities. The place was truly invented out of a passion for the wild and a belief that tourism could fund conservation.

Over the next decade, the careful development of naturalistic enclosures and the success in breeding several native species started attracting serious attention. Word-of-mouth spread, establishing Lanka Pub Zoo as a famous destination known not for spectacle, but for its genuine commitment to ecological integrity. Today, we honor his legacy by expanding this vision into a world-class facility.

A Force for Economic Growth

Recognizing the need for premium accommodations to support our global reputation, we have grown to include a 5-star hotel component. This expansion significantly improves the overall visitor experience and, critically, boosts the country's foreign dollar revenue, solidifying our role as a major national asset.
