Our Commitment

Lanka Pub Zoo is dedicated to conservation, education, and creating unforgettable experiences. Located in the heart of Maho, we provide a natural, caring habitat for our animals and serve as a vital center for wildlife preservation in Sri Lanka.

Education

Inspiring the next generation of conservationists.
